About Purrfect Paws

Our mission, story, and the team behind the shelter

Our Mission

At Purrfect Paws Adoption Center, our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome cats in need while promoting responsible pet ownership and compassionate care.

Founded in 2020, we've helped over 500 cats find their forever homes. We believe that every cat deserves a second chance at happiness, regardless of their age, breed, or background. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure each cat receives proper medical care, nutrition, and socialization while they wait for their perfect match.

We're committed to creating lasting bonds between cats and their adoptive families through careful matching, education, and ongoing support. Our adoption process is designed to ensure the best possible outcomes for both cats and adopters.

Our Story

Purrfect Paws began as a small foster network run by a group of passionate cat lovers who saw the overwhelming need for cat rescue in our community. What started in a spare bedroom has grown into a full-fledged adoption center with state-of-the-art facilities.

Our founder, Sarah Mitchell, started rescuing stray cats in her neighborhood after finding a litter of abandoned kittens. Word spread quickly, and soon she was receiving calls from all over the city. With the help of volunteers and generous donors, we opened our first shelter location in 2020.

Today, we operate a modern facility that can house up to 50 cats at a time, with separate areas for kittens, adult cats, and cats with special needs. We've built partnerships with local veterinarians, pet supply stores, and other animal welfare organizations to provide the best possible care for our feline friends.
